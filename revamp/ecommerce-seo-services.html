Expert Q&A

E-commerce SEO FAQs

How long does it take to see e-commerce SEO results?

Typically, noticeable growth in traffic and rankings starts within 3-4 months, with significant revenue impact seeing a "hockey stick" growth curve around months 6-9.

Do you work with Shopify and Magento?

Yes, we have dedicated specialists for Shopify, Magento (Adobe Commerce), WooCommerce, BigCommerce, and custom headless e-commerce builds.

What is LLM Optimization for e-commerce?

LLM (Large Language Model) optimization ensures your brand is mentioned as a top recommendation when users ask AI tools like ChatGPT "Which is the best [product category]?"

How do you handle technical SEO for stores with thousands of SKUs?

We use automated auditing tools and custom scripts to manage large-scale stores. Our focus is on faceted navigation, canonical tags, and XML sitemaps to ensure every product page is crawled and indexed efficiently.

Can you help with Google Merchant Center (GMC) suspensions?

Yes, our team specializes in GMC recovery. We audit your feed data against Google's policies, fix data quality issues, and handle the formal appeal process to get your organic shopping listings back online.

Do you provide content writing for product descriptions?

Absolutely. We have a team of e-commerce copywriters who create SEO-optimized, high-conversion product descriptions that follow E-E-A-T guidelines and improve user engagement.

FAQ

UI/UX Design FAQs

What is the difference between UI and UX design?

UX (User Experience) covers the overall feel and usability — research, flows, and structure. UI (User Interface) is the visual layer — colours, typography, and interactive elements. We provide both as an integrated service.

How long does a UI/UX design project take?

A standard web/app design project typically takes 4-8 weeks depending on complexity, number of screens, and revision cycles. We provide a clear timeline at project kick-off.

Do you provide developer handoff files?

Yes, we deliver production-ready Figma files with organised layers, a complete design system, assets in all formats, and an interactive prototype to guide developers through the implementation.

Can you redesign our existing product?

Absolutely. Product redesigns are one of our most common engagements. We conduct a UX audit of your existing product before designing a measurably better replacement.
